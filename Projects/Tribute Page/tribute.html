Julia Child Tribute

Julia Child

Here's a timeline of Julia Child's life:

  • 1912 – Was born in Pasadena, CA
  • 1934 – Graduated from Smith College with a history degree
  • 1946 – Married Paul Cushing Child in Lumberville, PA
  • 1948 – Moved to Paris
  • 1951 – Graduated from Le Cordon Bleu
  • 1951 – Started L'école des Trois Gourmandes (The School of the Three Food Lovers) to teach American women to cook with Simone Beck and Louisette Bertholle
  • 1961 – Published Mastering the Art of French Cooking
  • 1963 – Debuted The French Chef
  • 1981 – Founded the American Institute of Wine and Food
  • 1989 – Published her magnum opus, a book and video series entitled The Way to Cook
  • 1994 – Paul Child died not long after experiencing multiple strokes
  • 2000 – Received French Legion of Honour and was elected a Fellow of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences
  • 2001 – Moved to a retirement community
  • 2003 – Awarded the US Presidential Medal of Freedom
  • 2004 – Died of kidney failure in Montecito, CA
  • 2007 – Was inducted into the National Women’s Hall of Fame

"To be a good cook you have to have a love of the good, a love of hard work, and a love of creating.”

-Julia Child
